Sutable Crops in Jalgaon

The major crops produced in the district are banana (Jalgaon is the top banana producer in Maharashtra), cotton, sorghum, wheat, millet, lime, peanut, and sugarcane..

Banana

Banana is cultivated primarily for food and secondarily for the production of fibre used in the textile industry. Bananas are vigorously growing, monocotyledonous herbaceous plants. The cultivars vary greatly in plant and fruit size, plant morphology, fruit quality and disease and insect resistance. Most bananas have a sweet flavor when ripe; exceptions to this are cooking bananas and plantains.

favorable soil and climate-

Well drained loamy soils are suitable for banana cultivation. Alkaline and saline soils should be avoided.

Watering Frequency-

Banana is a water-loving plant. After banana plantation the soil should not be allowed to dry completely. Drip irrigation is most ideal for banana cultivation. Irrigation requirement of banana under drip irrigation varies from 5 l/d to 18.6 l/d/plant at early and peak growth stages of the plant respectively. The frequency of drip irrigation can be daily or alternate days.
